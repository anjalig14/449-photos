About Roses

Roses are commonly gifted to loved ones as a form of affection.
Roses symbolize love and romance but each color can have its own
meaning. Red roses symbolize romance and true love while pink roses
show gratitude and admiration. Yellow roses symbolize friendship and
white mean purity, youthfulness, and innocence. It is truly beautiful
the way that we try to find beautiful meanings to convey through flowers.
